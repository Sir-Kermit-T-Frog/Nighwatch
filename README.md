This is a fnaf like game but startrek themed
arrow keys are used to navigate main menu
press enter to select your difficulty



PHASER BUTTON:
Stuns the borg for 5 sec, you must remodulate the frequency after 2 uses, it has a cd of 10 seconds


FORCEFIELDS(In cameras)
Press the button to place a forcefield in the room, maximum two forcefields
Make sure to modulate the frequencies regularly, as the borg do adapt to them after to successful blocks


LIGHTS! 
If you dont turn these on, you cannot see which door the borg are at, you can tell when they are at a door based off of the walking cue

CAMERAS!
In order to switch to a different camera, you must press the number key corrosponding with the camera number

FORCEFIELDS! (bridge)
Press the buttons to close the door and prevent the borg from getting in, you do not need to modulate the frequencies in order for these forcefields to work

REBOOT:
Use arrow keys to navigate the menu, press enter to confirm an option.

HEADPHONES ARE STRONGLY RECOMMENDED FOR BEST EXPERIENCE
